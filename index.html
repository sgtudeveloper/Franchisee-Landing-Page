Indian nonprofit IGCSM empowers underprivileged with education, skill development, microfinance. ISO certified, collaborates for sustainable impact, reaching 50,000+ people since 2008. Education is one of the cornerstones of IGCSM’s mission. Recognizing that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty.

IGCSM has implemented various programs aimed at providing quality education to children and adults in underserved areas. These programs range from basic literacy classes to advanced computer training courses.

IGCSM's commitment to empowering students is further reinforced by the fact that all certificates and diplomas issued by the organization are 100% government-approved. This government recognition is a critical factor that significantly enhances the value and credibility of the qualifications offered by IGCSM.

Opportunity to join Government Initiatives

Joining IGCSM as a franchisee offers a unique and powerful opportunity to become an integral part of both national and international educational initiatives.
